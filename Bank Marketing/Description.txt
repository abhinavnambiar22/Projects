# Desciption of Bank Marketing Project

This project is a classification problem which decides whether a customer subscribes to a term deposit depending on a variety of features such as customer's details, account type, amount in bank, recent transaction details etc. specified in the dataset.
We used various models such as XGBClassifier, Random Forest and Simple Logistic Regression to compare their classification metrics.
The results are potrayed in the form of heatmaps for better representation.
